About Us

We are an open portal to everyone who loves animals. Doesn't matter if you are a shelter or a dog pound, a single or a family, if you want to give them a second opportunity this is your web.
Come on and join us!

Terms And Conditions

Firstly, responsibility is a must, the adoption would only be achieved by compromised and consequent people who love animals and treat them the best way.
Through the life of the adopted animals, we reserve the right to contact the adoption family to check and control the animal health and status.
If any harm or suffering is detected during the checking process, legal measurements will start.
Animals are not toys, they are extra members of the family that have to be treated as equals and that have to be loved, respected and taken care of always.
